Appraisal Review System

APPRAISAL REVIEW SYSTEM

Appraisal Review System is an evidence based system generating realistic performance evaluation and provides objective evidence of the same. It links real time vessel performance and evidence based training with the job description of an employee to provide unbiased competency profile.

It is a high impact system, based on data analysis, devoid of wide spread myths and blind faith in "the way we have always done". The system is customized to company's specific needs and requirements taking into account the specific requirements of the business and the crew.

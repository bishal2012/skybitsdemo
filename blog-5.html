AI in the retail industry

This is the time of artificial intelligence. Every industry is using AI. Retail industry is also using AI for benefits.AI is a smart way to categorize products. Chatbot is a popular application of AI in retail. Chat bots help retailers to provide great customer service, help customers find items on the site, notify them about new collections, and offer them apparel similar to things they have already chosen. Use of AI also helps in tracking the level of customer satisfaction. Using AI Some retail stores have already created cashier-less stores. These stores prevent theft.

In India, AI is not used by retailers that much. But there are many AI companies in India who are developing various new products and services using artificial intelligence. The Importance of AI and how it will help the country

Artificial Intelligence is an area of Computer Science which helps machines to act like human. AI gives machines the human intelligence. AI has various components. Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing these are some of the components of AI. Now a days AI is a very important part of any organization or any country. AI has many applications in various fields. Face or image recognition, Statistical analysis, chatbot for website, speech recognition etc. are the applications of AI.

More use of AI will make a country technologically powerful. India is also investing more in AI. AI can be used in different industries, like – health sector, financial sector, manufacturing industry, aviation industry, gaming zone. AI can make disaster relief more efficient. For the first time in 2015 earthquake of Nepal, AI was extensively used in relief and humanitarian aid. AI can be used to boost crop production. Companies like Skybits Technologies located in Kolkata specializes in various AI solutions and have served the banking and pharmaceutical industries.

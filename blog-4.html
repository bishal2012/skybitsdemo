Education Made Easy With AI

'AI is the best or the worst thing that can happen to humanity’- Stephen Hawking
So why not make the best use of it for bringing a change to the education system?

Artificial intelligence is extending its frontier in technology and knowledge. Wherever you see, people are discussing about machines with intelligence which improve our lives. A lot of applications and concepts remain highly technical and can be little confusing if you're not strong about the technology and it’s concepts. I am here to make you aware of the basics of artificial intelligence and help you conquer the AI world.

Artificial intelligence is believed to be the new spark of the modern era. In saying this, it is believed that AI will soon empower most of our activities in society and business by drastically changing the ways we work and live.

A profound impact of it is seen in the nature of services within the education sector. Empowering and updating of systems with AI has resulted in better impartment of knowledge and worthy evaluation of assessments by making them less of a blackbox as shared by biggies like Elon Musk.

In the context of emerging countries, an effective education sector is playing an important role in transforming the country through advanced technology teaching. Since, currently half of the population of India is below 25, the adoption of new digital means for improvement of education, are increasing. It is estimated that schools spent nearly USD 160 billion on education technology and India’s Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has decided to include Artificial Intelligence in their syllabus for school students as an attempt to mould the education system.

Nowadays, one can easily find video tutorials on any subject with a high quality resolution. The educational institutions are planning to introduce an AI based grading system to reduce the time spent on correcting papers and to make it error-free. With the advent of AI tools, the teachers can customize the courses according to the needs of the students. In fact, AI tutors are there also to serve as an additional guide to the students.

This is where companies like SkyBits Technologies, located in Kolkata, specialized in AI technologies are growing up. They have introduced content summarization and information extraction tools which can be used to improve the education system and make it simple to the students to understand the basics. They also have their in-house school ERP that make teaching more fun and interesting. Skybits have also developed a virtual chemistry lab that can be easily downloaded on every students laptop or cellphone. This will help students to complete lab experiments outside lab house and promotes safety culture amongst students. To know more about the methods, you can look into SkyBits

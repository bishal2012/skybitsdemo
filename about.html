About Company

Mission is to bring the power of AI to every business

SkyBits Technologies Pvt Ltd. was incorporated in October 2013 by Veterans of the IT industry with immense global experience to provide end to end mobility based solutions. In the very first year, SkyBits managed to get some top global clients across the world like Alchemy for which they developed a cloud based online training platform.

It continued to provide different non data science solutions to various companies in USA till 2015, where the company decided to take a strategic call on focusing mostly on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Solutions.

Over the years It has continued its excillence in the fields of machine learning and AI with achievements landing them with a MoU with IIEST was signed at New Delhi in the presence of our Honorable President Mr. Pranab Mukherjee (at that time) during the Industry Academia Interactive at Rashtrapati Bhavan on 4th Nov, 2015.

The goal is to help businesses grow, transform and achieve their business objectives. SkyBits specializes in Image Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Statistical Analysis and Mobile Application Technologies.

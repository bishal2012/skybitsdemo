Facial Detection For Enhanced Security

In this period of cruel business world, it is essential to keep your premises highly protected by investing in the right security technology. Good old method like hiring of a security team is still working, but there are more technical ways that you should pay attention to. Some of the ways like finger print or biometric are still being used in the companies but these technologies are no more difficult to hack so one of the most promising is a face detection technology that allows saving a budget while improving a security level.

So First of all let me tell you what is facial detection technology?

Various recognizable indicators of facial features are measured by facial recognition tech (FRT) from approximately 80 nodal points, creating a faceprint – a numerical code. Some of these features include length of the jawline, cheekbones shape, distance between the eyes and the depth of the eye sockets, and the nose width. The measurements gathered by the system are then put in a database and compared to other detected faces when a certain person stands before the camera.

HOW DOES FACIAL RECIGNITION TECHNOLOGY ACTUALLY WORK?

The mathematical algorithms of biometric facial detection follow different stages of image processing:

Capture
The first step is for the system to collect physical or behavioural samples in predetermined conditions and during a stated period of time.

Extraction
Then, all this gathered data should be extracted from the samples to create templates based on them.

Comparison
After the extraction, collected data is compared with the existing templates.

Matching
The final stage of face detection technology is to make a decision whether the face’s features of a new sample are matching with the one from a facial database or not. It usually takes just seconds.

We are already using the best facial recognition technology i.e. The Apple’s iPhone X which represents a beginning of the new era by implementing FRT(Facial Recognizable Technology) for unlocking a smartphone. It helps us in protecting our mobile phones from being theft.

There are more FR(face recognition) algorithms and techniques than there are companies. But with its huge database of images, Facebook’s algorithm has a leg up on most others in that it is constantly being taught how to improve.

The amazing uses of facial recognition tech are growing up each day. It is already being used for examination, investigation, and monitoring. Governments all over the world use it to identify potential and current threats. Retailers and businesses of all kinds look for suspected shoplifters or track their workers’ chronograms. There are a lot of social media apps which increase a user experience with FRT(Facial Recognition Technology).

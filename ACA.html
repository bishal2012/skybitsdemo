Automatic Credit Analyzer

AI Based Solution

SkyBits has been immensely involved with AI and the banking sector
for the last 5 years. After a detailed R&D for 2 years, we have come
up with a Credit Analyzer. It is an automated system developed with
NLP that analyzes the auditors report, the Directors report as well as
scans the internet for articles about a company prior to giving a risk assessment score for loan approval within a few minutes. This is a first
of its kind for the banking industry as the same work would take
weeks to complete.
